Our Story
Story & Philosophy

Founded in 2005 by Mohammad Iqbal Lone, Iqbal Coaching Institute (ICI) started in Parimpora with a mission: education as self-discovery, not just facts. We blend rigorous academics with holistic development, ensuring every student finds confidence and clarity.

Our motto drives us: "Know thyself, self-realization is half done." With 20+ years of experience, dedicated faculty, and a community-focused environment, we've become a trusted name in Srinagar.
